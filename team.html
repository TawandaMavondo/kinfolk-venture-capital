The Team

Jay Freeman

Founder & Managing Partner

Over the past 10 years, Jay has worked in the technology and venture ecosystem, with a focus on helping B2B, Consumer, Commerce, Fintech, Legal Tech, PAAS startups and large enterprises, to enhance product innovation, software development, and digital growth. Recently, Jay led Global Product Strategy and Management for PayPal across MNAs managing customer experiences and mobile growth.

Jay Freeman is also the Founder & CEO of Bourn.io, an AI-powered visual commerce platform that lets consumers buy whatever they see.

Additionally, he previously founded TinkerGrowth, a venture studio of product architects, designers, and engineers from Google, Nike, Uber, and Spotify. The collective is passionate about helping founders drive their mission, create meaningful product experiences, build sustainable businesses, and gain access to capital.

As a leader, Jay applies a strategic approach to product, design, technology, and growth. In his spare time, Jay gives back to his community as a Techstars mentor and Republic Venture Partner, where he provides guidance and support to new entrepreneurs and startups.

Originally from Boston, Massachusetts, Jay received his Bachelor of Arts in Business and Sociology from Brandeis University.
