The Kinfolk Story

The accumulation of navigational, linguistic, resistant, aspirational, and social capital has generated a rich well of cultural wealth that is untapped and has potential through thoughtful capital and adaptive leadership. Our goal is to design our family, celebrate cultural wealth, and harness the entrepreneurial energy of the continent to transform Africa and its perception in the world.

Kinfolk is a venture capital fund that reflects African values and combines them with the lessons found within the African diaspora. The early formative years of a company is when they are most receptive and impressionable and we encourage our portfolio companies to behave in ways that mirror our family morals, values, and principles.

  • Faith

  • Hard Work

  • Hospitality

  • Humility

  • Integrity

  • Courage

Our Debut Fund, a first in Africa, is solving a significant funding gap in the Seed to Series A stage. We created a model that reliably empowers early-stage entrepreneurs to build values-driven, sustainable, profitable enterprises.
